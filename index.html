WTA 2024 Analysis

A comprehensive analysis of the Women's Tennis Association 2024 Points Dataset, examining serve performance across different surfaces and tournaments.

Project Overview

This project analyzes point-by-point data from the 2024 WTA season, focusing on serve performance metrics and tournament statistics. The analysis includes data cleaning, statistical insights, and visualizations.

Key Findings

Serve Strength by Surface

  • Grass: Players hold serve about 70.5% of the time
  • Hard: Players hold serve about 66.0% of the time
  • Clay: Players hold serve about 61.6% of the time

Insights & Observations

  • Surface Advantage: The 9% difference in hold percentage between grass (70.5%) and clay (61.6%) suggests that surface characteristics significantly impact serve performance.
  • Tournament Distribution: Grand Slams account for the highest number of game-rows, indicating comprehensive coverage of major tournaments.
  • Hard Court Dominance: Hard courts represent the majority of games (33,830 out of 55,457), reflecting the prevalence in the WTA calendar.
